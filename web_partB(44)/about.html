OUR STORY

Welcome to d&b, a luxury clothing brand founded by two university engineering students, Dotan and Bar, driven by a shared passion for fashion and quality. At d&b, we believe that clothing is more than just fabric – it's an expression of individuality, confidence, and elegance. Our mission is to create high-end designs that combine premium craftsmanship with timeless style, offering a wardrobe that feels as exceptional as it looks.

We are committed to redefining luxury by blending meticulous attention to detail with innovative design, ensuring that every piece you wear tells a story of sophistication and care. Join us on this journey to celebrate quality, creativity, and the pursuit of perfection. D&B – where passion meets luxury.
